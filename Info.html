Sun Conure

•Lifespan: 15 - 30 years

•Size: Full grown up to 12 inches

•Diet: Fruits/flowers/berries
/buds/blossoms/seeds
/nuts and insects.

Fun Facts

Sun conures are well known for their beautiful striking colours.
Aside from its colour, it is also well know for its noisy and loud personality. Not one to be recommended as the apartment pet.
Bird owners looking to pet an exotic looking bird but can't afford to home a large big tend to get sun conure as its compatible subtitute.

African Grey Parrot

•Lifespan: 22 - 70 years

•Size: up to 13 inches

•Diet: Pellets/seeds/grains/sprouts
/fruits/vegetables/nuts/
flowers/supplements

Fun Facts

The african grey often fluffs up to look bigger as a form of defense.
It has a strong ability to mimic human speech, learning up to a stunning 1000 words !
They can be needy when it comes to emotional attention from its owner.
