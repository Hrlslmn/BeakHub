SEEDS

  • Too high in fat and fibre, Too low in vitamins and minerals, Health issues
  • Despite being a bird favourite food, always Watch out for seed portions.
  • Diet Suggestion: 10% or less of total meal portion.

FRUITS & VEGETABLES

  • Ensure vegetables/Fruits are washed and remove pits and seeds that might be harmful for the bird.
  • A good balance of vegetables and fruits is highly recommeded for birds health.
  • Diet Suggestion: 10% to 25% of total meal portion.

SPRAY MILLETS

  • Millets make good alternatives when it comes to rewarding your bird.
    Replace seeds and nuts rewards with millets that filled with nutrients.
