Common tricks bird owners teach thier birds

Play Dead
Place your parrot on your finger and push your finger up and over your parrot to make it lay on its back. While doing this repeatedly say action words like "play dead" then give your parrot a treat after recovering it back to its feet.
Bird Wave
1) Prepare a proper perch for your bird to sit on for comfort when learning this trick!
2) Get a wooden stick and allow bird to get a grip of it.
3) Upon gripping of the stick, wave the stick slowly in a hand wave manner and say "hello".
4) Give a treat to your bird after a few rounds of waving and when the time comes your bird will learn to respond to "hello" with a wave.
Dancing
--Ensure that you are the person/owner your bird is close to or closest with for ease of teaching, otherwise there is going to be a huge barrier to teach.--
Pick a music.
1) Choice of music is important as it will be the indicator for the bird to dance and it will get used to the music and dance along easily.
2) As you are playing the music, move your head up and down and slowly your bird will follow the motion and start dancing.
3) In a week or 2 your bird would get the hang of it .Some birds might even start dancing at random times
